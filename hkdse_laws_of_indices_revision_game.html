Laws of Indices

  • Multiplication: \(a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n}\)
  • Division: \(\frac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n}\)
  • Power of Power: \((a^m)^n = a^{mn}\)
  • Power of Product: \((ab)^n = a^n b^n\)
  • Negative Index: \(a^{-n} = \frac{1}{a^n}\)
  • Zero Index: \(a^0 = 1\)
